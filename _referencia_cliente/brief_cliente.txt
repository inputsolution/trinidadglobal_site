About paragraphs


Trinidad Global LLC is incorporated in Wyoming, United States, with a defined operating purpose and a documented connection to established commercial operations in the Dominican Republic. It serves as the international procurement and trade support entity of the Trinidad automotive ecosystem.



The company supports global sourcing, supplier relationship management, trade documentation, import coordination, and logistics support for affiliated automotive operations.



Trinidad Global LLC is a business-to-business entity. It does not operate as a retail business, ecommerce marketplace, financial services provider, investment vehicle, or consumer-facing automotive service company.



By establishing a U.S.-based structure, the Trinidad automotive ecosystem gains a clearer international counterpart for supplier relationships, trade coordination, logistics partners, and standard corporate onboarding processes.

Global Procurement Management

Import & Logistics Coordination

Supplier Relationship Management


Managing commercial relationships, supplier onboarding, distribution discussions, and long-term supply agreements with international brands, distributors, and manufacturing partners.



Commercial Trade Support


Supporting purchase documentation, supplier onboarding, order coordination, and corporate trade records related to documented international procurement activities for affiliated automotive operations.

8. Limpieza técnica obligatoria
Eliminar completamente cualquier triple backtick ``` o marca de Markdown.

Guardar el archivo final como index.html.

Verificar que todo el CSS esté limpio dentro de <style>.

No dejar URLs en formato Markdown, por ejemplo:

[https://www.trinidadglobal.com](https://www.trinidadglobal.com)

debe ser:

https://www.trinidadglobal.com

No usar CSS anidado inválido como:

.footer-brand { h4 { ... } }

debe ser:

.footer-brand h4 { ... }

Mantener Netlify Forms sin onsubmit.

No agregar JavaScript que intercepte o bloquee el envío del formulario.

Mantener:

<form name="contact" method="POST" data-netlify="true" netlify-honeypot="bot-field">

Verificar que todos los campos del formulario tengan atributo name.

Verificar responsive en móvil, especialmente 320px de ancho.

Verificar que no exista scroll horizontal en móvil.

Confirmar que los enlaces internos del menú funcionen:

#about, #business, #ecosystem, #suppliers, #compliance, #contact.

<div class="rule"></div>
<div class="about-grid">
<div class="about-text fade-in">
<p>
Trinidad Global LLC was incorporated in Wyoming, United States, with a defined operating purpose and a documented connection to established commercial operations in the Dominican Republic. It serves as the international business and procurement vehicle of the Trinidad automotive ecosystem.
</p>
<p>
The company supports <strong>global sourcing, supplier relationship management, import coordination, and documented supplier payment coordination</strong> on behalf of its affiliated operating company in the Dominican Republic.
</p>
<p>
Trinidad Global is not a consumer-facing business, a financial services provider, or an investment vehicle. It is a structured, operational platform designed to consolidate and formalize the international dimension of an existing and established commercial ecosystem.
</p>
<p>
By establishing a U.S.-based entity, the Trinidad automotive ecosystem gains access to international supply chains, credible counterparty infrastructure, and the compliance framework expected by global suppliers, logistics providers, and financial institutions.
